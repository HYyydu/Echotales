# Privacy Policy for Echotales

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

Echotales ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create an account or sign in, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Display name** - Used to personalize your experience
- **Authentication tokens** - When using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In
- **User ID** - A unique identifier for your account

### 2. Audio Data
- **Voice recordings** - When you choose to use voice cloning features, we collect and process audio recordings of your voice
- **Microphone access** - Required only when you actively choose to record your voice
- These recordings are sent to our third-party service provider (ElevenLabs) for voice cloning purposes

### 3. Reading Activity
- **Reading history** - Books you view, including title, author, genre, and timestamp
- **Downloaded books** - Record of books you've downloaded for offline reading
- This data helps provide personalized recommendations and track your reading progress

### 4. Document Content
- **User-uploaded documents** - PDF, EPUB, TXT, and Word files you choose to import
- These documents are processed locally on your device and stored in our cloud storage for your access across devices

### 5. Technical Information
- **Device information** - iOS version, app version
- **Error logs** - Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
   - Store and sync your reading library across devices
   - Process voice recordings for voice cloning
   - Generate personalized audio narration

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Track reading history to provide personalized recommendations
   - Remember your preferences and settings
   - Provide customer support

3. **Maintain Security**
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- **Firebase Authentication** - Account credentials
- **Firebase Firestore** - User profiles, reading history, and metadata
- **Firebase Storage** - Book files and audio recordings
- **ElevenLabs** - Voice cloning data (see Third-Party Services below)

### Security Measures
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- We use Firebase's security rules to protect your data from unauthorized access
- Audio recordings are processed securely and deleted after voice model creation

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

### Firebase (Google)
- **Purpose:** Authentication, database, and file storage
- **Data Shared:** Email, name, user ID, reading history, uploaded documents
- **Privacy Policy:** https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google Sign-In
- **Purpose:** Authentication
- **Data Shared:** Email, name, profile information
- **Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Apple Sign-In
- **Purpose:** Authentication
- **Data Shared:** Email (optional), name (optional), user ID

### ElevenLabs
- **Purpose:** Voice cloning and text-to-speech generation
- **Data Shared:** Audio recordings, voice clone names

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### Modify Your Data
- Update your display name in the app settings
- Modify your account information
- Delete individual reading history entries

### Delete Your Data
- Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
- Delete individual voice recordings
- Clear your reading history

### Data Portability
Request your data in a portable format.

### Withdraw Consent
- Revoke microphone permissions in iOS Settings
- Sign out of your account at any time
- Delete voice clones you've created

## Children's Privacy

Echotales is designed to be family-friendly and includes content for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
- Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
- We recommend parental supervision for children using the app

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Reading history:** Retained until you clear it or delete your account
- **Voice recordings:** Retained until you delete them or your account
- **Voice models:** Retained by ElevenLabs according to their retention policy
- **Deleted data:** Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.
